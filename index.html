About Me

Creative Developer & Visual Artist

I'm Sanele Ngubane, a passionate web developer who is also a graphic designer, photographer, and motion graphics artist using After Effects. Growing up in the rural areas of Eastern Cape, Mt Frere, I developed a deep love for coding and tech designs.

I studied Interactive Media at NEMISA, where I honed my skills in combining technical expertise with creative vision. My work spans across multiple disciplines - from building responsive websites and web applications to creating compelling visual identities and capturing stunning photographs.

My journey from the rural Eastern Cape to becoming a multi-disciplinary creative professional drives my passion for creating work that not only looks beautiful but also tells meaningful stories and solves real problems.
