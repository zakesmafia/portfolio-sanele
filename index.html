About Me

Creative Developer & Visual Storyteller

I'm Sanele Ngubane, a multidisciplinary creative professional specializing in web development, graphic design, motion graphics, and photography. Based in South Africa, I combine technical expertise with artistic vision to create compelling digital experiences.

With over 5 years of industry experience, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and established brands to bring their visions to life. My approach blends aesthetic sensibility with functional design principles to deliver solutions that are both beautiful and effective.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new photography techniques or experimenting with animation styles. I'm passionate about continuous learning and staying at the forefront of digital design trends.
